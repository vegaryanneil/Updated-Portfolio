Values I believe in.

  • Making things fun and easy. As a lifelong overachiever, I’m learning to let go of the idea that anything worth achieving requires hard work and long hours. I do my best (and most profitable!) work when I let things be fun and easy. I also love that what’s fun and easy for me is different from what’s fun and easy for you.
  • Leading uncomfortable conversations. I started my own business because I wanted to work only with people I wanted to be friends with, because I wanted to put relationships before sales. The thing about real relationships is that we have to be willing to be vulnerable and say what’s really on our minds and in our hearts.
  • Creating diverse and inclusive spaces. I’ve lived my entire life as a foreigner and I’ve been shaped by multiple cultural influences. I know what it feels like to feel out of place everywhere, so I’m passionate about creating spaces where everyone feels like they belong. I bring my experience working as a Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion leader in the corporate world to the online business space, with the message that the way you choose to live is not the only way.
